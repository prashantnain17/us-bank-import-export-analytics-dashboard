# 📊 US Bank Import/Export Analytics

## 🔍 Overview
An interactive two-page Power BI dashboard analyzing 52,000+ 
US Export-Import Bank financing deals from FY2007–FY2025.
Dataset sourced from US Data.gov (official US government 
open data portal).

## 📌 Key Features
- Approved Amount Trend Analysis (FY2007–FY2025)
- Top Destination Countries by Financing
- Program-wise Deal Distribution (Guarantee, Insurance, 
  Loan, Working Capital)
- Decision Authority Breakdown (Board, Individual, 
  Loan/Credit Committee)
- Geographic Financing Map (World Map Visual)
- Disbursement Rate KPI
- Interactive Slicers for Country, Program & Fiscal Year

## 📈 Key Insights
- United States & Mexico dominate as top financing 
  destinations
- Financing activity peaked in FY2010–2012 post 
  financial crisis
- Guarantee programs account for 52.5% of all 
  approved financing
- Board-level authority approves the highest value 
  individual deals
- Insurance programs show consistent growth since FY2018
